### Project Description
SoloSafe is a smart travel planner designed for solo travelers to plan trips safely. It combines trip planning, emergency contact management, nearby service lookup, and a smart checklist to ensure safe and organized travel.

### The Problem statement
Solo travel is exciting but risky. Solo travelers often face safety concerns, lack proper planning tools, and need quick access to emergency contacts and nearby services. Managing itineraries, checklists, and local information can be stressful.


### The Solution
Providing a smart safety checklist

Storing and alerting emergency contacts

Showing nearby hospitals, police, hotels, and restaurants

Offering an organized trip itinerary dashboard

Highlighting risk levels for destinations

## Features

List of key features:
- Smart Safety Checklist: Tracks essential travel safety steps with progress.
- Emergency Contacts: Alerts and calls emergency contacts in critical situations.
- Nearby Services: Shows nearby hospitals, police stations, hotels, and restaurants using Google Maps URLs.
- Risk Level Indicator: Shows destination safety level as Low, Medium, or High.
